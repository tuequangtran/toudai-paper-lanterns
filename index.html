Over 500 years of experience. A celebrated history.

The history of our company begins in Uji, Japan. In the year 1400, our founder and the very first paper lantern master created the very first lantern under the 灯台 brand, as a personal gift to the emperor. From that moment, our company has gone from strength to strength, customizing paper lanterns for important events year after year.

We hand make all our products. See for yourself.

We seamlessly combine modern machinery with traditional techniques, handmaking all our paper lanterns with centuries of artisinal pride. Each of our product comes with centuries of craftsmanship, handed down through the centuries from master to apprentice.
